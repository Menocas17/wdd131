Site Name:

PFJ Nicaragua Sur

This name is the official name of the FSY of my region in Nicaragua, the FSY is focused on helping the youth between 16 to 18 years old to come unto Christ
Optional domain availabitily: pfjnicaraguasur.com

Site Purpose:

This website will provide the general information about the PFJ campament, you'll also find information about the staff and a gallery of photos related to the PFJ, also will include a web app to manage the registration of the campament and will have a section of resources for the staff.

Scenarios:

- Examples for staff members:

What are the resources available for counselours?

What is the agenda for the PFJ 2024 week

Where can I find the info of the parcipants of X company?

- Examples for general public:

Where is going to take place the campament?

What are the dates for the campament?

How can I get in contact with the PFJ staff?

How do I register in the campament?

Color Schema:

  • Main Color = #4545cc, it will be used in the navegation bar and in the footer also in some headings in the page.
  • Secondary Color = #8717EB, it will be used for headings (h2) and for backgrounds.
  • Contrast Color = #8717EB, it will be use for buttons and hovers.
  • Secondary Contrast Color = #fe0074, it will be used for buttons and hovers.
  • Light Grey = #F8F7F8, it will be used for the entire body background.
  • Grey = #EDECED, it will be used as contrast in some menu's section.

Typography:

- Roboto

This font is going to be the general font used in the body, this sitplan uses the font as an example

- Gagalin

This font is going to be used in the titles (h1, h2, h3), you can see it on the titles of this web page
